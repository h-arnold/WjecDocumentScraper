GCSE

Speaking and listening assessment dates: The dates for 2025 GCSE Welsh Second
Language Non-Examination Assessments are as follows – Unit 1 (3020U10) 18, 19 and 20
March 2025; Unit 2 (3020U20) 25, 26 and 27 March 2025. Sample submission date on
Surpass plus relevant paperwork: 5 May 2025.

A new look to Unit 2 papers: In 2025 the Unit 2 papers will appear with a change to the
usual formatting. Visit our subject page GCSE Welsh Second Language (wjec.co.uk) ‘Key
Documents’ and ‘Oral Examination and Coursework Forms’ tabs to see an example of the
new formatting design.

IAMIS Upload: In 2025, centre assessed NEA moderation samples (Unit 1 3020U10 & Unit
2 3020U20) will be uploaded via IAMIS. Centres will be able to input marks and upload
samples on the same system and will not need to wait for key codes. More information will
be provided via a circular in September.

Oral Handbook 2025: Visit our subject page GCSE Welsh Second Language (wjec.co.uk)
‘Key Documents’ and ‘Oral Examination and Coursework Forms’ tabs to see a copy of the
Oral Handbook.

Visit the secure website (Portal) to read the moderator's report on the work of your
centre's units 1 and 2 in the summer 2024 series.

Examination dates 2025: Unit 3 (3020U30) – 14 May 2025 (am); Unit 4 (3020U40) – 2
June 2025 (am)

Blended Learning Resources: Visit our Digital Resources website on the WJEC website to
find a series of blended learning lessons/resources based on Units 1-4 GCSE Welsh Second
Language.
