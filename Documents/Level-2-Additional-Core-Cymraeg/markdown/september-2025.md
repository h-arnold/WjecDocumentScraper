GCSE

Speaking and listening assessment dates: The dates for 2026 GCSE Welsh Second
Language Non-Examination Assessments are as follows – Unit 1 (3020U10) 10, 11 and 12
March 2026; Unit 2 (3020U20) 17, 18 and 19 March 2026. Sample submission date on
IAMIS plus relevant paperwork: 5 May 2026.

Oral Handbook 2026: Visit our subject page GCSE Welsh Second Language (wjec.co.uk)
‘Key Documents’ and ‘Oral Examination and Coursework Forms’ tabs to see a copy of the
Oral Handbook.

Visit the secure website (Portal) to read the moderator's report on the work of your
centre's units 1 and 2 in the summer 2025 series.

WJEC will no longer send out hard copies of Unit 1 DVDs (from 2026 onwards).
Examination Officers will need to download the visual materials digitally from Portal
(secure website) on the dates specified in the oral handbook.

Examination dates 2026: Unit 3 (3020U30) – 13 May 2026 (am); Unit 4 (3020U40) – 2
June 2026 (am)

Blended Learning Resources: Visit our Digital Resources website on the WJEC website to
find a series of blended learning lessons/resources based on Units 1-4 GCSE Welsh Second
Language.
