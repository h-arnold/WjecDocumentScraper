We would like to wish you well at the start of a new year in your departments. In this newsletter you
will find information to remind you exactly what we have on our subject pages, Portal and key dates
in the coming months.

GCSE

Speaking and listening assessment dates: The dates for 2025 GCSE Welsh Second Language Non-
Examination Assessments are as follows – Unit 1 (3020U10) 18, 19 and 20 March 2025; Unit 2
(3020U20) 25, 26 and 27 March 2025. Sample submission date on IAMIS Upload plus relevant
paperwork: 5 May 2025.

A new look to Unit 2 papers: In 2025 the Unit 2 papers will appear with a change to the usual
formatting. Visit our subject page GCSE Welsh Second Language (wjec.co.uk) ‘Key Documents’ and
‘Oral Examination and Coursework Forms’ tabs to see an example of the new formatting design in
our Oral Handbook.

IAMIS Upload: In 2025, centre assessed NEA moderation samples (Unit 1 3020U10 & Unit 2
3020U20) will be uploaded via IAMIS. Centres will be able to input marks and upload samples on the
same system and will not need to wait for key codes. Please visit our subject page, ‘Key Documents’
and ‘Oral Examination and Coursework Forms’ tabs to see GCSE Welsh Second Language IAMIS
Upload guidelines.

Oral Handbook 2025: Visit our subject page GCSE Welsh Second Language (wjec.co.uk) ‘Key
Documents’ and ‘Oral Examination and Coursework Forms’ tabs to see a copy of the Oral Handbook.

Visit the secure website (Portal) to read the moderator's report on the work of your centre's units 1
and 2 in the summer 2024 series.

Examination dates 2025: Unit 3 (3020U30) – 14 May 2025 (am); Unit 4 (3020U40) – 2 June 2025
(am)

Blended Learning Resources: Visit our Digital Resources website on the WJEC website to find a
series of blended learning lessons/resources based on Units 1-4 GCSE Welsh Second Language.

GCE – AS/A Level

IAMIS Upload: In 2025, centre assessed NEA moderation samples (Unit 2 2020U20) will be
uploaded via IAMIS. Centres will be able to input marks and upload samples on the same system and
will not need to wait for key codes.

GCE Welsh Second Language Oral Examination Dates: The 2025 dates for the GCE Welsh Second
Language Oral Examinations are as follows – Unit 1 (2020U10) and Unit 4 (1020U40): 24 March – 9
May 2025.

Date of submission of Unit 2 samples on IAMIS Upload together with relevant paperwork: 15 May
2025.

Visit the secure website (Portal) to read the moderator's report on the work of your centre's Unit 2
in the summer 2024 series.

Examination dates 2025: Unit 3 (2020U30) – 21 May (pm); Unit 5 (1020U50) – 2 June
(pm); Unit 6 (1020U60) – 12 June (am)
