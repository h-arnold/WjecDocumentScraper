GCSE Iaith a Llenyddiaeth Gymraeg - Teaching from 2025

The specification has been published on our GCSE Iaith a Llenyddiaeth Gymraeg Welsh website and
the Sample Assessment Materials were published on 19 December 2024 on our new subject website.
Briefings  were  held  during  November.  We  will  be  carrying  out  face-to-face  training  on  the  new
specification regionally, in South, North and Mid Wales. Further details of the dates can be found here.

The registrations for the Regional DP days will be collected by your Headteacher. Registration will
not be possible through WJEC's website.

We will publish a Guide to the Exam and Non-Exam Assessment during the Summer Term 2025.The
Power points will be published on the WJEC Portal.
